# Who are the Queens of The West?

**Find Out!** We play the last Sunday of the month at **The Catfish Bar** from 4:00 - 6:00pm, 30 Gertrude St, Fitzroy. 
*Our next gig is 23th November, 4:00pm - 6:00pm and features Aneta Graham.*

![Aneta Graham](/images/Aneta.png)

The Queens are a band featuring rich, 3-part, sassy, all-girl harmonies flavoured with jazz, blues and humour; these Melbourne Westies are fast becoming a musical tour de force.

The girls convinced four assorted Punk, Rock, Jazz, and Blues instrumentalists that they would enjoy playing behind three harmonising singers,and a ukulele.

We've never looked back.

To get in touch with us, check our [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/QueensOfTheWest).
